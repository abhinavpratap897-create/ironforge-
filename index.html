What is IRONFORGE?

IRONFORGE is a single HTML file that works as a full-featured gym tracker. Open it in any browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox — and it runs completely offline. No logins, no subscriptions, no data sent anywhere. Everything you track is stored privately in your browser's local storage.

It's designed for Indian gym-goers who want a serious, no-nonsense tool: personalised workout splits, Indian meal plans in your budget, body composition tracking, and expense management — all in one dark, fast, distraction-free interface.

8 Modules — How to Use Each
⚙️
1. Setup
START HERE FIRST

Fill in your name, age, weight, height, and gender. Pick your fitness goal (Lean, Bulky, Strength, etc.) and experience level. Set your gym fee, supplement budget, and how many days a week you train. This powers everything else — your split, macros, and calorie targets are all calculated from this.

📊
2. Dashboard
YOUR DAILY HQ

See your today's workout split auto-generated from your days/week setting. Check off exercises as you complete them — once all are ticked, the workout is automatically logged. Track your 7-day streak, this week's session count, and your daily macro progress bars.

⚡
3. Workout
INSTANT PLANS, NO API

Tap a quick preset (Push, Pull, Legs, Upper, Core, HIIT) for an instant plan, or use the custom generator — pick muscle group, duration, and equipment. Sets and reps are auto-assigned from your goal profile. The plan types itself out using a typewriter animation.

🥗
4. Diet
INDIAN MEALS IN YOUR BUDGET

Select veg / non-veg / vegan, enter your daily food budget in ₹, and list any allergies. IRONFORGE picks 5 Indian meals (Poha, Dal Rice, Chicken Curry, etc.) that fit your diet type, budget, and calorie target. Also use the food log to manually track what you actually eat.

📈
5. Progress
TRACK YOUR BODY

Log your weight, body fat %, chest, waist, arms, and legs measurements over time. A bar chart auto-builds from your last 14 entries. Your BMI, TDEE, and lean mass are always shown. Add personal bests — IRONFORGE alerts you when you set a new PR on any lift.

💰
6. Finance
SPEND SMARTER

Log gym fees, supplement purchases, and other fitness expenses. See your monthly spend vs budget with visual health bars, and your year-to-date total. Color-coded: red for gym, gold for supplements, gray for other.

📅
7. Calendar
VISUALISE YOUR CONSISTENCY

Browse any month to see which days were scheduled, completed, or missed. Click a past workout day to toggle it as done. See your full weekly split in the sidebar. The done/missed counters help you spot patterns in your consistency.

📚
8. Exercises
REFERENCE LIBRARY

Browse all 48 exercises across Chest, Back, Legs, Shoulders, Arms, and Core. Each card shows the setup, execution, and a coaching cue. Filter by muscle group. Use it mid-workout when you're unsure about form on an unfamiliar exercise.

Quick Start in 3 Steps
1
Go to Setup → fill in your profile
Your name, body stats, goal, days per week, and budget. Takes 2 minutes. This is required before any other page works correctly.
2
Open Dashboard → check off today's workout
Your split for today is auto-calculated. Tick exercises as you complete them. All done = workout logged. Your streak counter starts building.
3
Use Workout + Diet generators daily
Generate a workout plan with one click. Generate a meal plan matched to your calories and budget. Log your food, log your lifts, watch the progress page fill up over time.
🔒
100% PRIVATE — YOUR DATA NEVER LEAVES YOUR DEVICE

IRONFORGE makes zero network requests (except loading Google Fonts once). All your measurements, expenses, workouts, and meal logs are stored in your browser's localStorage. To back up your data, use your browser's export tools or simply copy the HTML file — your data lives in the browser profile, not the file.
